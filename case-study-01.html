Case Study 01

Webinar Landing Page Optimization

Increase webinar registrations through UX redesign
Context
  • High-value webinar with low conversions due to unclear messaging and friction.
Problems
  • Unclear value proposition
  • Weak trust signals
  • Long registration form
  • Low urgency
Strategy
  • Two-column layout with right-side form
  • Stronger value framing
  • Added social proof
  • Simplified fields
